Autotune Settings

? Enables automatic tuning of hashrate based on system conditions.
? Sets the maximum power limit in watts. Prevents power consumption from exceeding this threshold.
? Sets the maximum fan speed limit in percent. Ensures fans do not exceed this speed to control noise and wear.
? Maximum allowed power overshoot in watts. Provides a buffer for temporary spikes above the power limit.
? Maximum allowed fan speed overshoot in percent. Provides a buffer for temporary spikes above the fan limit.
? Maximum voltage for the ASIC in millivolts. Prevents over-voltage conditions that could damage hardware.
? Maximum frequency for the ASIC in megahertz. Prevents overclocking beyond safe limits.
? Maximum temperature for the ASIC in degrees Celsius. Ensures thermal safety to prevent hardware damage.
? Sets the maximum allowable Voltage/Frequency ratio. This ratio is crucial for determining efficiency and power delivery to ASICs. A higher ratio can lead to overheating, while a lower one might not provide enough power.
? Sets the minimum allowable Voltage/Frequency ratio. This ratio affects energy efficiency and chip performance. Setting this too low could cause insufficient power supply, leading to performance degradation or system instability.
